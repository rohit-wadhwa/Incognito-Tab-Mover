Incognito Tab Mover

Use Alt+M to move tabs

To use this extension, you must enable "Allow in Incognito" mode:
  1. Open chrome://extensions/
  2. Find Incognito Tab Mover and click Details
  3. Toggle on “Allow in incognito”

Enabling this extension in Incognito mode allows it to operate fully, ensuring functionality and privacy.
